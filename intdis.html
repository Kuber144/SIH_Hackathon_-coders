Intellectual Disability

Home for the MR above the age of Fourteen

Homes for the mentally retarded were established through the Non Governmental Organizations in the 7 Districts of Tiruvallur, Kancheepuram, Dindigul, Madurai, Coimbatore, Tirunelveli and Tiruchirapalli to benefit 40 Mentally Retarded Persons. During the year 2011-12, 10 Homes were started at Chennai, Cuddalore, Ramanathapuram, Salem, Sivagangai, Thanjavur, Namakkal, Erode, Thoothukudi and Kanyakumari for Men and 10 Homes were started at Chennai, Dindigul, Coimbatore, Krishnagiri, Theni, Tiruvannamalai, Kanchipuram, Madurai, Trichy and Tirunelveli for Women. The main Objectives of the Scheme is to provide shelter and support through Vocational Training Programmes to Mentally Retarded Persons above the age of 14 years. From the year 2014-2015 another 6 Homes are started at Dharmapuri, Karur, Nagapattinam, Perambalur, Pudukottai and Tiruvarur for Men and virudhunagar,Ariyalur,Trippur,Nilgris and villupuram. Totally 38 homes are available for the persons with Intellectual Disability in Tamil Nadu State.

Homes for the Mr girls above the age of Fourteen

To protect the adult Mentally Challenged girls, non Governmental Organisations are identified and given grants-in-aid to run 4 Homes at Salem, Tuticorin, Sivaganga and Vellore. Each Home maintains 30 girls.

Early Intervention Services for Mentally Retarded

Early Intervention Centres for Mentally Retarded were established in 31 districts to benefit 50 Children each. These Centers were established through NGOs. The main objective of these centers is to identify Children with Mental Retardation and children with allied disabilities at birth and provide early intervention services including early detection and identification (0-6years), with support and training to parents and families/caregivers to maximize the childâ€™s daily living skills, socialization, Cognitive and communication skill etc.

Maintenance Allowance to MR with 45 percentage

Maintenance Allowance of Rs.1,500/- per month to the mentally retarded persons with a disability percentage of 45% and above are eligible for this benefit. The maintenance allowance is sent to bank account of an individual directly from the treasury.

Provision of Weaning food to all EIC Children

Children with disabilities in the Early Intervention Centers are provided with nutritious meal as being provided in the Aaganwadi centers of Integrated Child Development Programme. Transport Charges are paid to implementing NGOs to bring food from nearby Anganwadi Centers. As many as, 2500 children with disabilities in 68 Early Intervention Centers are benefitted under this scheme.

Education Tour to Govt Special School Students

The State Government is taking a series of steps to improve the quality of education in the Government Schools of the State. The Government is committed to provide an atmosphere that is conducive to all-round growth & development of children. It is felt that education is imparted not only through formal classroom teaching but also through interaction with other children and exposure to new places of interest. Hence, traveling and study tours constitute an important source of imparting education to children. Needless to say that children can be empowered to appreciate and relate theory with practice in a better manner through educational tours and exposure.
