Muscular Dystrophy

Financial assisstance under Rashtriya Arogaya Nidhi scheme

Financial support upto Rs. 20 lakh under the Umbrella Scheme of Rashtriya Arogaya Nidhi shall be provided by the Central Government for treatment, of those rare diseases that require a one-time treatment . Beneficiaries for such financial assistance would not be limited to BPL families, but extended to about 40% of the population, who are eligible as per 23 norms of Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, for their treatment in Government tertiary hospitals only.

Maintenance allowance to persons affected with muscular dystrophy

The Maintenance Allowance given at the rate of Rs. 1,500/- per month to persons affected with Muscular Dystrophy. Those who are affected with 40% and above are eligible. The allowance is sent through ECS to bank account of individual directly from Treasury.
